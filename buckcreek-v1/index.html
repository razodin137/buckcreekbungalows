Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Buck Creek Bungalows unique?

Buck Creek Bungalows is a solar-powered lakefront property with direct water access, multiple viewing decks, and modern amenities. We're just minutes from Somerset while offering peaceful seclusion and immediate boat ramp access.

What water activities are available?

We provide complimentary canoes, paddle boards, and life vests. The property has direct lake access via a lit walkway, and there's a boat ramp adjacent to the property for easy launching.

What outdoor amenities are included?

The property features volleyball/badminton courts, multiple viewing decks, a fire pit, ample parking, and direct lake access. We also provide water equipment like canoes and paddle boards.

What is your minimum stay requirement?

We offer flexible booking options with a minimum stay of two nights for short-term rentals. We also offer both weekly discounts and affordable monthly rates for extended stays.

How many guests can the property accommodate?

The property comfortably sleeps up to 11 guests with 3 bedrooms, 5 beds, and 2 bathrooms.

Is the property family-friendly?

Yes! We're very family-friendly with activities for all ages, including water sports, outdoor games, and spacious common areas. We provide safety equipment for water activities.

How far are you from Somerset?

We're conveniently located just minutes from Somerset, offering easy access to restaurants, shopping, and entertainment while maintaining our peaceful lakefront setting.

What's parking like at the property?

We offer ample free parking on premises, with space for both vehicles and boat trailers.

Is there easy water access?

Yes! We have a lit walkway directly to the water and are adjacent to a boat ramp. It's just a two-minute walk from the house to the water.

What modern amenities are included?

The property features WiFi, smart TV, keypad entry, modern appliances, washer/dryer, and a fully equipped kitchen with BBQ facilities.

Is there internet access?

Yes, we provide complimentary WiFi throughout the property.

Is the property solar-powered?

Yes! We're proud to be powered by grid-tied solar, meaning your stay is powered by the sun while maintaining reliable power access.
