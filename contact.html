Common Questions

Quick answers to our most frequently asked questions

What's included in the rental?

All bungalows include full kitchen amenities, linens, towels, Wi-Fi, smart TV, outdoor grill, fire pit, and complimentary water sports equipment.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

We have a 2-night minimum stay requirement for weekends and a 3-night minimum for holiday weekends.

Are pets allowed?

No pets, and please don't feed any wildlife.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 30 days before check-in. Please contact us for specific policy details and special circumstances.
